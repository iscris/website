Knowledge Graph

The Data Commons Knowledge Graph is constructed by synthesizing a single database from many different data sources. This knowledge graph can be used both to explore what data is available and to understand the graph structure.

You can start looking around from some well known starting points:

  • Cities: Belo Horizonte, São Paulo, More ...
  • Administrative Capitals: Brasília, Rio de Janeiro
  • States: Minas Gerais, São Paulo, Amazonas, More ...
  • Countries: Brazil, United States, Australia, More ...
